FIX INCOMPLETE PAYMENT FLOW - 15 MINUTE DEADLINE

PROBLEM:
- Student clicks "Confirm" → sees payment screen → goes back without paying
- Booking shows "Pending Payment" in Instructor Panel
- Student Panel: booking disappears completely (NO way to return and pay)

SOLUTION:

1. STUDENT PANEL - ADD "PENDING PAYMENTS" SECTION:
   - Show all unpaid bookings
   - Display: Instructor name, lesson date/time, amount to pay
   - Add "Complete Payment" button for each booking
   - Add "Cancel Booking" button

2. BOOKING STATUSES:
   - "pending_payment" → Student confirmed but didn't pay yet
   - "paid" → Payment completed, booking confirmed
   - "cancelled" → Cancelled or expired

3. PAYMENT TIMEOUT - 15 MINUTES:
   - Set 15-minute deadline to complete payment
   - Show countdown timer: "Complete payment within 14:35"
   - Auto-cancel booking after 15 minutes if not paid
   - Release instructor's time slot back to available immediately

4. "COMPLETE PAYMENT" BUTTON:
   - Retrieve existing booking details
   - Show payment summary again
   - Direct to payment gateway
   - After payment: move to "Upcoming Lessons"

5. INSTRUCTOR PANEL:
   - Separate "Confirmed Lessons" (paid) from "Pending Payment"
   - Show 15-minute countdown for pending bookings
   - Auto-remove when cancelled/expired
   - Time slot becomes available again after auto-cancel

6. NOTIFICATIONS:
   - Student: "Complete your payment within 15 minutes or booking will be cancelled"
   - Student: Warning at 5 minutes remaining
   - Instructor: "Booking confirmed" (when paid) or "Booking cancelled - payment not completed" (if expired)

DATABASE:
- Add booking_status field: pending_payment / paid / cancelled
- Add payment_deadline timestamp (created_at + 15 minutes)
- Run cron job every minute to auto-cancel expired bookings
- Auto-cancel query: UPDATE bookings SET status='cancelled' WHERE status='pending_payment' AND payment_deadline < NOW()

TESTING:
1. Create booking → Go back without paying → Check "Pending Payments" appears with countdown
2. Click "Complete Payment" → Verify payment works
3. Wait 15 minutes → Verify auto-cancellation and slot becomes available
